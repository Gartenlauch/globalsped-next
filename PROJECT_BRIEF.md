# Project Brief

Globalsped is an international logistics company for transports between Europe, Central Asia, Caucasus and Middle East.

Goal:
Create a modern, premium, multilingual website that generates transport inquiries.

Primary target groups:
- Export companies in DACH
- Companies from Europe shipping goods to Central Asia, Caucasus and Middle East
- B2B logistics decision makers

Main conversion:
Transport request via /de/anfrage

Website style:
Premium, trustworthy, experienced, international, clear, professional.
